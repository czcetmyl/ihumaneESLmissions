A school day along the Tonlé Sap River
📖 Read the Story

Every day I go to school by the river. My school has one big classroom.

My teacher writes on the board. We learn letters and numbers.

At lunch I eat rice with my friends.

In the afternoon we read books.

I like learning new English words.

When I go home I teach my mother what I learned.

She smiles and says I am her smart child. 💙

❓ Activity 1 — Comprehension Questions

Answer each question in a full sentence.

1. Where is the school?
2. What does the teacher do?
3. What do they eat at lunch?
4. What does the child do when they go home?
5. How does the mother feel? Why?
✍️ Activity 2 — Write About YOUR School Day!

Write 3 sentences about your own school day. What do you do? What do you like?
