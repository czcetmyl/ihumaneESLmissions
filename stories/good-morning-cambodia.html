A morning in Cambodia — from sunrise to school
📖 Read the Story

The sun comes up over the river. Mia wakes up early.

She says good morning to her mother.

She washes her face and eats breakfast.

She puts on her school uniform.

She walks to school with her sister.

Her teacher says hello.

It is a good morning in Cambodia. ☀️
