A floating village on the Tonlé Sap Lake, Cambodia
📖 Read the Story

Our village floats on the water. Our houses are on boats.

When the river rises, our village moves.

We fish every day for food. Children play on the water.

At night the stars reflect in the river.

It is a different life. But it is our life.

We are proud of our floating home. 💚

📚 Vocabulary — 5 Important Words
floating
Staying on top of water without sinking. Our village is floating!
reflect
When something shows an image, like a mirror. Stars reflect in the river.
proud
Feeling happy and good about yourself or your home.
rise
To go up. The river rises when it rains a lot.
village
A small community where people live together.
