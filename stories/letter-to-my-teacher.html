Writing a letter is a beautiful way to say thank you
📖 Read the Model Letter
Dear Teacher,

My name is Linh. I am in Grade 3. I want to say thank you for teaching me English.

Before, I did not know any English words. Now I can read and write. I can say hello to people from other countries.

You are a very kind teacher. I work hard every day because of you.

I hope I can speak English very well one day.

Your student,
Linh 💛
🔍 Activity 1 — Parts of a Letter

Every letter has 3 parts. Can you find them in the letter above?

📩 Greeting
How you start the letter. You say the person's name.
Example: "Dear Teacher,"
📝 Body
The main part. You say what you want to say.
Example: "Thank you for teaching me..."
✍️ Closing
How you end the letter. You write your name.
Example: "Your student, Linh"
📚 Vocabulary
dear
A kind word we use to start a letter. "Dear Teacher" means "I care about you."
grateful
Feeling thankful for something good someone did for you.
improve
To get better at something. "I want to improve my English."
practice
To do something again and again to get better at it.
sincerely
A formal and honest way to close a letter.
