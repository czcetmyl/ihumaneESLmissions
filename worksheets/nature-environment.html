🔗 Activity 2 — Match the Word to Its Description

Read the description. Write the correct nature word on the line.

🌊 river
A large flowing body of fresh water. Children in Cambodia live near this.
☀️ sun
A very bright ball in the sky that gives us light and warmth every day.
🌳 tree
A tall plant with a trunk, branches, and leaves. Birds live in them.
☁️ cloud
White or grey shapes in the sky. They can bring rain.
🌸 flower
A beautiful plant that grows in the ground and has colorful petals.
