The beautiful animals of Cambodia
📖 Read the Story

Cambodia has many beautiful animals. The elephant is big and strong. It lives in the forest.

The fish lives in the Tonlé Sap River. There are many kinds of fish.

The bird flies high in the sky. The monkey climbs tall trees.

All these animals are important. We must take care of them. 🌿

📝 Activity 2 — Vocabulary Matching
🐘 elephant
A very large gray animal with a long nose (trunk). It is the largest land animal.
🌳 forest
A large area of land covered with many trees.
🐟 fish
An animal that lives in water and breathes through gills.
🐦 bird
An animal with wings and feathers that usually flies.
💪 strong
Having great power or force. An elephant is very strong.
⚠️ important
Something that matters very much and should be cared for.
