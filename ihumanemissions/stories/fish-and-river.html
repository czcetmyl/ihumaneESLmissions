Nemo the fish in the Tonlé Sap River, Cambodia
📖 Read the Story

My name is Nemo. I am a small fish.

I live in the Tonlé Sap River. The river is big and blue.

Every morning I swim and play.

I see the sun in the water.

I have many friends here.

I love my river home. 💙

📚 Vocabulary — 10 Words
name
What you are called. "My name is Nemo."
small
Not big. A little thing or animal.
fish
An animal that lives and swims in water.
river
A long flowing body of water.
big
Large in size. The opposite of small.
blue
The color of water and the sky.
swim
To move through water using your body.
play
To have fun with friends.
friends
People or animals you like and spend time with.
love
To like something or someone very much.
