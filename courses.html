🎓 Note for Parents & Guardians: All online classes are delivered live by IHM volunteer teachers through Messenger and Google Meet. Friday and Saturday night classes (8:30–9:00 PM Cambodia Time) are open to children joining from their homes or community centers. Monday to Friday, classes are held in person at the school built by International Humanitarian Missions along the Tonlé Sap River. No prior English knowledge is required to join our Beginner class.
